E14

Anchor-nav scrollspy

Top pickeffort M

Now — the anchor band underlines on hover only; on long pages (/vision has six sections) you never know where you are.

Proposed — the existing underline persists beneath the section currently in view and slides between labels as you scroll. Continuous orientation; the page reports your position like an instrument. Builds entirely on the already-shipped underline.

Deliberately not proposing

  • Custom cursor / cursor dot — reads startup-landing-page, not Nasdaq-listed fund; the play already lives in how surfaces respond, not in the pointer itself.
  • Scroll-pinned scenes / scroll-jacking — explicitly barred by the brief, and rightly so.
  • Animated gradients (home intro, CEF band) — paint-heavy, violates the transform/opacity rule for marginal effect.
  • News auto-advance carousel — motion the user didn't ask for, on content they're trying to read.
  • Odometer-style digit flips on count-ups — the current count-up already says "data"; digit flips say "slot machine".
  • Anything on filings, disclaimers, or FAQ risk copy — legal surfaces stay still.
