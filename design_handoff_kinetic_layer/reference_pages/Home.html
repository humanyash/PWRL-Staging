Introducing Powerlaw Corp.

A curated portfolio of private technology companies that you probably already know. Now available in one stock, tradable on Nasdaq. Powerlaw Corp. (Nasdaq: PWRL) is the flagship fund from Powerlaw Capital Group, an asset management firm building a publicly-listed platform for private technology investing.

We believe the world’s most transformative companies should be within everyone’s reach.

Powerlaw Corp. (Nasdaq: PWRL) seeks to provide investors with exposure to leading private technology companies through a single Nasdaq-listed security. PWRL is a listed closed-end fund registered under the Investment Company Act of 1940. It provides daily liquidity, monthly NAV reporting, quarterly portfolio disclosure, independent governance, and the disclosure standards of a registered investment company.

A History of Firsts

Powerlaw Corp. (Nasdaq: PWRL) is the first product of Powerlaw Capital Group and managed by Powerlaw Fund Adviser, LLC. The firm is backed by Akkadian Ventures' 16-year heritage in institutional private-company secondary markets.

One of the First in Secondaries

Ben Black and Pete Smith launched Akkadian Ventures to buy direct secondary shares from employees, founders, and early investors – when the market was still < $20B and largely unproven.

Scaling the Secondary Market

Akkadian closes its latest and largest flagship fund: $276M Fund VI to continue building one of the leading direct-secondary platforms in the market.

Expansive Innovation Engine

PWRL begins trading on the Nasdaq Stock Market as the first product of Powerlaw Capital Group. The US Venture Capital Market now exceeds $1.24 Trillion.

RAISE Global Established

Akkadian creates RAISE Global, which becomes one of the leading LP–GP emerging manager conferences connecting forward-thinking allocators with next-generation venture talent.

Powerlaw Capital Group Launches

Powerlaw Capital Group emerges as a platform with the goal of bringing exposure to leading private technology companies into the public market for investors globally.
